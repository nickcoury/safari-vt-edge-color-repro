Background: Transparent on Root Group. The ::view-transition-group(root) container gets background: transparent. This is the group element that contains the image-pair — setting its background might affect the area behind the snapshot images, including safe area regions.
::view-transition-group(root) { background: transparent; }
View Transition Card

The ::view-transition-group(root) is the container that wraps the old/new image pair. It covers the full viewport. If Safari uses this element's background to fill safe areas outside the snapshot bounds, transparent might prevent the white flash.

This is different from targeting the old/new pseudo-elements — the group is the outer container, so its background might be what bleeds into the safe area regions.
