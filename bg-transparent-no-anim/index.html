Variant 13
Combo: Transparent BG + No Root Animation. Combines variant 2 and variant 9 — root old/new get both background: transparent and animation: none. The root snapshots never paint visibly (no animation) and even if they did, their background is transparent.
::view-transition-old(root), ::view-transition-new(root) { background: transparent; animation: none; }
View Transition Card

Double protection: the root snapshots don't animate (so they shouldn't paint), and even if Safari composites them anyway, their background is transparent instead of white.

If this doesn't fix the flash, neither the animation timing nor the background color of the root pseudo-elements controls the safe area bar color.
